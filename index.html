Profile

Kentaro Ozeki is a postdoctoral researcher in philosophy at the University of Tokyo (2024-2026). He obtained his PhD in philosophy from Keio University in 2023. His research interests include metaphysics, logic, and Austrian philosophy. He is currently working on the applied theory of nonexistent objects and interdisciplinary studies in philosophy and informatics.
